About Marijke Woodruff

Executive Advisor | Fractional CHRO | Business Transformation & People Strategy Leader

Marijke Woodruff

Authentic Leader. Culture Architect. Strategic Growth Partner.

Marijke O'Connor Woodruff is an award-winning executive leader, strategic business partner, and trusted advisor with more than 20 years of experience helping organizations accelerate growth, lead transformation, and build high-performing cultures.

As the founder of M. Consulting, Marijke believes that people are the greatest catalyst for business success. A business leader first, she partners with CEOs, founders, executive teams, and boards to align people strategy with business strategy — creating organizations that are agile, innovative, and positioned for sustainable growth.

Her expertise spans strategic HR leadership, organizational effectiveness, workforce strategy, leadership development, change management, mergers and acquisitions, culture transformation, and HR digital enablement. She is recognized for translating complex business challenges into practical, measurable solutions that improve organizational performance, strengthen leadership capability, and deliver long-term value.

Marijke has successfully led organizations through rapid growth, executive transitions, organizational redesign, cultural transformation, and M&A due diligence and post-acquisition integration. She combines operational discipline with strategic vision, helping organizations harmonize people, processes, technology, and culture while ensuring employees remain engaged and aligned throughout periods of change.

A strong advocate for leveraging technology and artificial intelligence to enhance — not replace — the human experience, Marijke helps organizations modernize HR, improve decision-making, and create scalable systems that drive productivity and business results.

Community & Purpose

Beyond the boardroom, Marijke is deeply committed to servant leadership and community engagement. She served for more than a decade on the American Red Cross Board of Directors, where she received a Leadership Award in recognition of her service and impact. She is also an active volunteer and committee member for Croquet for a Cure, supporting Alzheimer's research and care initiatives.

Marijke is passionate about the power of storytelling to inspire change. She serves as a producer of the documentary Untethered: A Wildly Different Normal, a film that explores connection, resilience, intentional living, and redefining success through a more meaningful lens.

She earned her Bachelor of Science in Communication from Bradley University, where she was a four-time National Speech Champion. She graduated high school from The Academy of the Holy Names in Tampa, Florida, where she was inspired to live their motto, "Esse Quam Videri" — to be, rather than to seem.

Above all, Marijke is a wife, mother, sister, mentor, and lifelong learner. She finds inspiration through yoga, gardening, photography, travel, and exploring new cultures — experiences that continue to shape her perspective as both a leader and a human being.

Purpose

Marijke's purpose is to help organizations and leaders build momentum — unlocking the full potential of their people, strengthening leadership, and creating cultures where innovation, collaboration, and performance thrive. Guided by curiosity, inspired by possibility, and grounded in authentic relationships, she helps organizations navigate change with confidence while turning bold ideas into sustainable results.
