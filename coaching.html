Coaching & Leadership Development

Navigating Change With Clarity, Confidence, and Direction

Most people seek coaching during a transition. A new role. A career decision. A leadership challenge. A team conflict. A family shift. Or simply the sense that life is asking more of them than they currently know how to carry.

Transitions are not just logistical — they are identity shifts.

How we think, communicate, make decisions, and relate to others often has to evolve before our circumstances can. My coaching is designed to help individuals and leaders understand themselves more clearly, lead more intentionally, and move forward with steadiness rather than reaction.

This work blends practical guidance, leadership development, and the Enneagram — a powerful framework for understanding motivation, behavior patterns, stress responses, and growth pathways.

Leadership Development & the Enneagram

The Enneagram is not a personality label — it is a leadership awareness tool.

It helps identify core motivations, communication patterns, blind spots, conflict triggers, stress responses, leadership strengths, and growth pathways. Leaders often discover that their greatest strengths and their greatest leadership challenges come from the same internal patterns.

Using the Enneagram, we work on leadership maturity, emotional intelligence, decision-making, managing pressure, understanding team members, leading diverse personalities, and improving influence and trust.

Outcome: Leaders who understand both themselves and others — which is the foundation of effective leadership.

Who This Is For

My coaching is a strong fit for individuals who are thoughtful, motivated, and ready to engage honestly in their own growth. This work may be right for you if you are:

  • Entering the workforce and want guidance navigating professional expectations
  • Considering a career change or feeling misaligned in your current role
  • Stepping into leadership and want to lead well — not just manage tasks
  • A leader navigating team dynamics, communication challenges, or conflict
  • Experiencing a life or identity transition and want clarity in your next steps
  • Feeling stuck in repeating patterns in work or relationships
  • Wanting to better understand yourself through the Enneagram and apply it practically
  • Seeking a trusted, objective sounding board outside of your organization or family system

You do not need to have everything figured out. You only need to be willing to look at what is true and move forward intentionally.

Who This Is Not For

Coaching is not therapy, crisis counseling, or a substitute for mental health treatment. This work may not be the right fit if you are:

  • Looking for someone to make decisions for you
  • Wanting a quick fix without personal effort
  • Hoping to change other people without examining your own patterns
  • Primarily needing clinical mental health support or trauma processing
  • Seeking legal, financial, or employment representation
  • Unwilling to have candid conversations or consider feedback

My role is not to tell you what to do. My role is to help you think clearly, understand your patterns, strengthen your leadership, and make aligned decisions you can stand behind.

What You Can Expect

Sessions are conversational, structured, and practical — combining reflection with real-life application and next steps.

  • A confidential, judgment-free space
  • Honest but respectful feedback
  • Practical tools you can use immediately
  • Reflection combined with real-life application

Many clients begin coaching because of a specific challenge. They often continue because of the clarity and steadiness they gain in all areas of life.
