From Campus to Career

Coaching for College-to-Workplace Success

This 5-session coaching program is designed to support recent college graduates in successfully transitioning from academic life to the professional workplace. Through strategic, individualized coaching, participants will gain the skills, mindset, and strategies necessary to secure employment and thrive in their early careers.

$500
Complete 5-Session Package

Each session is approximately 60 minutes, with light follow-up resources and messaging support between sessions.

Program Objectives

  • Empower college students and recent graduates with career readiness skills
  • Provide a structured pathway to build professional confidence
  • Develop personalized networking and job search strategies
  • Enhance communication and interview performance
  • Establish accountability to drive job search momentum and success

The 5-Session Coaching Experience

Session 1

Get to Know You & Set Goals

  • Career interest inventory and assessment review
  • Identify short-term and long-term career goals
  • Define success metrics and create a coaching roadmap
Session 2

Networking Strategy

  • Develop a targeted list of locations, industries, roles, and contacts
  • LinkedIn profile review and optimization
  • Outreach templates and informational interview strategies
Session 3

Interview Preparation

  • Mock interview with real-time feedback
  • STAR method coaching and storytelling techniques
  • Addressing gaps, transitions, and post-college experience
Session 4

Communication Coaching

  • Email etiquette, professional language, and body language coaching
  • Elevator pitch refinement
  • Role-playing for difficult conversations (salary negotiation, conflict resolution)
Session 5

Follow-Up & Accountability

  • Progress review and barrier identification
  • Adjustment of strategies and re-prioritization
  • Creation of a 30/60/90-day job or internship search action plan

Program Outcomes

By the end of the 5 sessions, participants will:

  • Identify personal strengths, career goals, and job market positioning
  • Develop a targeted networking and job search plan
  • Be prepared to effectively navigate interviews and follow-up communication
  • Strengthen professional style, communication and workplace readiness skills
  • Create an action-oriented follow-up and accountability plan

Return on Investment

Measurable outcomes that demonstrate program impact

Job Placement Rate

% of participants employed within 3 months post-program

Interview Conversion Rate

% of participants moving from interview to offer stage

Client Confidence Score

Pre- and post-program self-assessment of confidence in job search and workplace readiness

LinkedIn Engagement

Increase in profile views, recruiter messages, and connections post-program

Resume Engagement

Metrics such as email callbacks, interviews scheduled, or application feedback
